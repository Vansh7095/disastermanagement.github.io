Vansh Sharma - Innovator Behind DisasterShield AI

Hi, I'm Vansh Sharma, a passionate student with a vision to revolutionize disaster management using cutting-edge technology. This project, DisasterShield AI, was born from my curiosity in electronics, AI, and sustainable development.

This system is capable of monitoring multiple environmental conditions and alerting users in real time, helping to prevent or mitigate the impact of disasters. I designed and coded both the hardware system and this website.

My goal is to make this platform open and scalable so that it can be deployed in real-world scenarios, schools, towns, and even remote villages. I believe science and technology should be accessible and functional for the betterment of society.
